Figure and Figcaption Example

The figure element is used to encapsulate any content considered an independent unit, such as images, graphics, diagrams, photographs, code, etc. The figcaption element is used within the figure element to provide a caption or subtitle to this encapsulated content. This helps provide context or additional information about the figure.
