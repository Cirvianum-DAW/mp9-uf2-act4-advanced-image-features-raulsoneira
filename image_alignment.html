The map and area elements

Adding the CSS property max-width to images automatically resizes them to fit the width of the parent element without exceeding their original size. This is useful for large images displayed on smaller devices such as mobile phones or tablets, ensuring that the image will be fully visible without requiring horizontal scrolling.
